Curriculum Vitae

Jinhan Kim is a fifth-year Ph.D (integrated M.S and Ph.D program) student at COINSE Lab in KAIST. He generally works in the area of software engineering, currently focusing on mutation testing, fault localisation, and deep learning system testing. He received his B.S from School of Computing, KAIST, and started Ph.D from 2017 under the supervision of Dr. Shin Yoo.
